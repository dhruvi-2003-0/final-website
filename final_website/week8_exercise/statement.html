    The Story of Mahatma Gandhi

    The project, called "Gandhi: The Journey of a Mahatma," aims to go above traditional teaching techniques by providing a single, immersive look at Mahatma Gandhi's life. Inspired by my own experience of rediscovering Indian history after relocating to Canada, this project aims to serve as a means of connecting users to the extensive biography of Gandhi and his significant role in achieving India's independence.

    In a gentle way, you can shake the world.

    "Gandhi: The Journey of a Mahatma" is an educational project that aims to inspire and inform, not just a digital one. The website seeks to bring history to life by utilizing interactive web design and narrative storytelling, providing visitors with a unique ability to look into the life and legacy of Mahatma Gandhi. This initiative invites visitors to go on an enlightening and transforming journey of discovery by providing a bridge between the past and present. Finally, this proposal presents a concept for a website that offers a full, interactive biography of Mahatma Gandhi's life, going beyond traditional educational methods.
